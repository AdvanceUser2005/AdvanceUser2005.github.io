About Me

A bit about how I work

Blacksburg, VA

I'm a Computer Engineering student at Virginia Tech (Class of 2028), specializing in robotics and autonomous systems. I like building things that exist in the physical world and respond intelligently to it — the messier the integration between hardware and software, the more I enjoy it.

Software engineer on the Competitive Robotics Organization (CRO) team at Virginia Tech — where most of my real engineering education has happened, outside the classroom.

Right now I'm building Mini Tesla: a regenerative electric vehicle prototype exploring propulsion, drivetrain gearing, and energy recovery from the ground up. Before it, I shipped Lunar ResQ and Mining Mayhem — two competition robots running full ROS 2 autonomy stacks.

When I'm not writing firmware, I'm running my Prusa MK4S, doing PCB work, or out shooting photos.
